Curbside Pickup

Enjoy the convenience of our curbside pickup service. Simply place your order online and pick it up at your scheduled time!

How It Works

  1. Place your order online
  2. Choose a pickup time
  3. Park in a designated curbside pickup spot
  4. Call the number on the sign
  5. We'll bring your groceries right to your car!
